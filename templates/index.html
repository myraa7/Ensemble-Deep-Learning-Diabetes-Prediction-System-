An Innovative Ensemble Deep Learning Clinical Decision Support System for Diabetes Prediction

ABOUT US



Diabetes is a significant global health concern, with an increasing number of diabetic people at risk. It is considered a chronic disease and leads to a significant number of fatalities annually.



Early prediction of diabetes is essential for preventing its progression and reddata1ng the risk of severe complications such as kidney and heart diseases. This study proposes an innovative Ensemble Deep Learning (EDL) clinical decision support system for diabetes prediction with high accuracy.
